What are Oscar Predictors ?

Oscar Award has has always been the heated topic in every February. Countless assumptions  will arise with regard to who or which film is the last winner.  Based on past data, the five most accurate predictors are: 

British Academy of Film and Television Arts (BAFTA)
Screen Actors Guild Awards (SAG)
The Critics' Choice Movie Awards
New York Film Critics Circle Awards
Golden Globe Awards
