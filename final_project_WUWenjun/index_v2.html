What are Oscar Predictors ?

Oscar Award has always been the heated topic in every February. Countless assumptions  will arise with regard to who  is the last winner.  Based on past data, the five most accurate predictors are: 

British Academy of Film and Television Arts (BAFTA)
Screen Actors Guild Awards (SAG)
The Critics' Choice Movie Awards
Golden Globe Awards
